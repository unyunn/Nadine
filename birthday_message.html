About Nadine Zahrani

You're a mystery that never ceases to amaze. They say you like to eat scorpions-honestly, I don't know if that's a myth or a fact, but if it's true, then you're brave. But if not, well... at least you managed to make me believe. I know, the past leaves traces that are hard to erase. Trust issues are not just words, but wounds that don't heal with "time will heal everything." I'm not here to convince you that the world is always good, or that everyone can be trusted. I'm just saying that you don't have to go through everything alone. Like Summer in 500 Days of Summer, you have your own path, and I'll never be the one to force it. I just hope that wherever you go, you find happiness that is truly yours. If one day you look back, I hope what you remember is not fear, but that there was once someone who genuinely wished you well.
